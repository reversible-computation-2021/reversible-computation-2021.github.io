Due to the current health situation and travel regulations in the world, RC2021 will be held online. Participation is free of charge. Registered participants and speakers will receive Zoom information to the email address used for registering. Please click the button below.

Registration Closed

By clicking the button, you will be directed to an MS-office form. After completing registration, you will receive a message from 'yuen.shoji' at c0.nagoya-u.jp to the email address used for registartion. Please reply back to confirm the email address is correct. For authors, Springer may send the (physical) LNCS proceedings. If you like to have one, please enter your postal address at registration.

Registration will be open until the end of the conference. (Authors who like to have the LNCS proceedings, please let us know before the conference starts. We will let Springer know the postal addresses right after the conference.)

If you have any problem for registration, please contact info@reversible-computation.org
